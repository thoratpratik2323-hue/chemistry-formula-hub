Solutions

Mole Fraction (\(\chi\))
\(\chi_A = \frac{n_A}{n_A + n_B} \quad | \quad \sum \chi = 1\)

Dhyan rakhna Ishu, sum hamesha 1 hi hoga.

Molarity vs Molality
\(M = \frac{n}{V_{(L)}} \quad | \quad m = \frac{n}{W_{solvent(kg)}}\)

Molarity temperature pe depend karti h, molality nahi!

Henry's Law
\(P = K_H \cdot \chi\)

Solubility of gas in liquid.

Raoult's Law
\(\frac{P^0_1 - P_1}{P^0_1} = i \cdot \chi_2\)

Relative lowering of vapor pressure (RLVP).

Colligative Properties
\(\Delta T_b = i K_b m \quad | \quad \Delta T_f = i K_f m\)

Elevation of BP & Depression of FP.

Osmotic Pressure (\(\pi\))
\(\pi = i CRT\)

The most important property for Boards calculations.

Electrochemistry

Nernst Equation
\(E_{cell} = E^0_{cell} - \frac{0.059}{n} \log \frac{[Anode]}{[Cathode]}\)

Always check 'n' (number of electrons exchanged).

Gibbs Energy & Equilibrium
\(\Delta G^0 = -nFE^0_{cell} = -2.303 RT \log K_c\)

Spontaneous reaction ke liye \(\Delta G\) negative hota h.

Molar Conductivity (\(\Lambda_m\))
\(\Lambda_m = \frac{\kappa \times 1000}{M} \quad | \quad \kappa = G \cdot \frac{l}{A}\)

Units dhyan se: \(S \, cm^2 mol^{-1}\).

Kohlrausch's Law
\(\Lambda^0_m = \nu_+ \lambda^0_+ + \nu_- \lambda^0_-\)

Infinite dilution pe har ion ki conductivity.

Faraday's Laws
\(w = ZIt = \frac{E}{F} \cdot It \quad | \quad \frac{w_1}{w_2} = \frac{E_1}{E_2}\)

1 Faraday = 96500 C approx.

Chemical Kinetics

Zero Order
\(kt = [R]_0 - [R] \quad | \quad t_{1/2} = \frac{[R]_0}{2k}\)

Rate concentration pe depend nahi karta.

First Order
\(k = \frac{2.303}{t} \log \frac{[R]_0}{[R]} \quad | \quad t_{1/2} = \frac{0.693}{k}\)

Board exams ka sabse favorite formula!

Arrhenius Equation
\(\log \frac{k_2}{k_1} = \frac{E_a}{2.303 R} \left[ \frac{T_2 - T_1}{T_1 T_2} \right]\)

Temperature change se rate constant kitna badlega.

Collision Theory
\(k = P \cdot Z_{AB} \cdot e^{-E_a/RT}\)

P is steric factor (proper orientation).

Coordination

Magnetic Moment (\(\mu\))
\(\mu = \sqrt{n(n+2)} \, BM\)

n = no. of unpaired electrons. Units: Bohr Magneton.

CFT Splitting (\(\Delta_o\))
\(CFSE = [-0.4x + 0.6y]\Delta_o + mP\)

Octahedral field mein d-orbitals ka splitting.

EAN (Effective Atomic No)
\(EAN = Z - (ON) + 2(CN)\)

Stability check karne ke liye kaam aata h.
